Legal terms and use

Terms of use

IndiCare supports professional practice. It does not replace professional judgement, policy, supervision, or safeguarding procedures.

  • Use outputs responsibly and review before saving or sharing.
  • Do not rely on the assistant as the sole basis for urgent safeguarding decisions.
  • Follow local procedures, management advice, and emergency routes where required.

Privacy

Use only information that is necessary and proportionate for the task. Avoid excessive personal data. Handle records in line with organisational policy, data protection requirements, and ICO principles.

Intellectual property

IndiCare OS, its workflows, structure, prompts, branding, and interface remain protected. Internal copying, reuse, or adaptation should follow the permissions set by the owner of the platform.
