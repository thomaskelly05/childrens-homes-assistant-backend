Add Daily Note

Create a therapeutic, child-centred daily record aligned to the Quality Standards.

Record Details

1. Views, Wishes and Feelings

Record the young person’s voice clearly. What did they say, communicate, request, avoid, or appear to feel today?

2. Relationships and Emotional Safety

Reflect on interactions with staff, peers and others. Note moments of connection, co-regulation, trust, conflict or repair.

3. Education

Record learning engagement, attendance, barriers, support offered and any educational achievements.

4. Enjoyment and Achievement

Capture positives, strengths, interests, successes and enjoyable moments from the day.

5. Health and Well-being

Note physical health, sleep, food, medication impact, emotional wellbeing or any health concerns.

6. Family, Identity and Important Relationships

Record family time, significant contact, identity-related matters and their emotional impact.

7. Positive Behaviour Support and Regulation

Describe behaviour support using therapeutic, non-judgemental language. Include triggers, regulation needs and what helped.

8. Activities, Routines and Care Planning

Record participation, routines, structure, transitions and how care planning was reflected in the day.

9. Safeguarding, Reflection and Next Steps

Record any concerns, reflection on support provided, and clear next actions for the team.

Final AI Review

Use AI to review the full note for therapeutic language, spelling, child voice, strengths-based wording and Quality Standards coverage.
