Residential AI support for real children’s home practice

IndiCare brings together your operational workspace and a broad residential AI assistant that can help adults think clearly, write well, organise information, improve documentation, and support calm, practical, professionally grounded care practice.

Open Assistant Open Journal Open Supervision Open AI Notes
The Assistant is your main AI workspace. Use it for general residential questions, drafting support, reflection, planning, wording, documentation review, support-plan thinking, handovers, summaries, and professional sense-checking.

How the tools are separated

  • Assistant is the general residential AI brain for broad support and questions.
  • AI Notes is a separate tool for meeting-note workflows and specialist note generation.
  • Reports and Documents are structured production tools, not the whole assistant.

Main workspaces Routes

Assistant Ask general residential care questions, improve wording, build drafts, reflect on practice, and organise documentation. Staff Journal Create and review reflective staff journal entries and development records. Supervision Open supervision records, review submissions, and support staff development work. AI Notes Use your separate AI note-taking workflow for meeting notes, templates, and structured saved outputs.
