2. Reflective Question Bank

  • What stands out most for you?
  • What was happening inside for you?
  • Which values felt present or challenged?
  • What might support you now?

3. Shift Check‑In / Check‑Out

Check‑In

  • How are you arriving today?
  • Anything you want to park?
  • What value do you want to hold?

Check‑Out

  • What stays with you?
  • What went okay?
  • What can you hand over?

4. PACE Micro‑Interventions

Grounding

Acceptance

Curiosity

Empathy
