# Ofsted SCCIF Overview (Summary)

The SCCIF for children's homes focuses on whether children are helped and protected, whether their experiences and progress are good, and whether leaders provide effective care and oversight.

Inspectors look for child voice, quality of care and support, how staff help and protect, and leadership impact. Evidence should show action and difference made, not paperwork alone.

This is a short practice summary — not a substitute for the official inspection framework.
