# Safeguarding Practice Principles

Safeguarding reflection should follow Working Together principles: early help, proportionate response, multi-agency cooperation, and child-centred practice.

ORB does not make threshold decisions. Escalate immediate risk through local procedures. Remind users to follow their home's safeguarding policy and designated lead.

Record concerns factually, what was observed, what the child said, actions taken, and who was notified.
