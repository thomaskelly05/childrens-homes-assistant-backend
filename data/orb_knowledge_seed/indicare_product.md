# IndiCare Product Context

IndiCare is a residential children's homes operating system and intelligence platform. It is designed to support staff and managers with care recording, safeguarding oversight, Ofsted/SCCIF readiness, actions, governance, workforce support, documents, chronology and reflective practice.

ORB Care Companion is the standalone assistant layer at /orb — a ChatGPT-style, voice-enabled companion for general questions, recording quality, Ofsted/SCCIF reflection, safeguarding thinking and therapeutic practice. It does not access live OS records.

IndiCare OS ORB at /assistant/orb is the operational, permissioned assistant that may use OS and Care Hub context where your role allows.

Platform areas include Care Hub (command centre), Record, Young People, Chronology, Documents, Actions, Intelligence Spine, Ofsted readiness, workforce support, governance, Reports and ORB.
