# Quality Standards Overview (Summary)

The Children's Homes Quality Standards sit under the Children's Homes (England) Regulations 2015. They set expectations for how homes protect, care for, and support children.

Standards cover enjoyment and achievement, protection, health and wellbeing, education, relationships, home environment, leadership and management, and care planning.

Link everyday practice and records to how the home meets these standards in lived experience, not checklist compliance alone.
