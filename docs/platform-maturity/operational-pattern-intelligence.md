# Operational pattern intelligence

Pattern intelligence must be deterministic and review-led. It should surface repeated operational signals without making speculative predictions.

## Inputs

- chronology history
- lifecycle history
- audit history
- evidence review history
- sign-off history
- inspection gap history
- operational queue history

## Approved language

- "Repeated operational gap"
- "Recurring safeguarding concern"
- "Repeated unresolved review"
- "Pattern may require oversight"

Avoid diagnostic, predictive or certainty-based wording.

## Future architecture

Pattern services should aggregate replayable historical data by provider, home, child, record type and timeframe. Frontend cards should show counts, age, unresolved status and linked evidence rather than generated conclusions.
