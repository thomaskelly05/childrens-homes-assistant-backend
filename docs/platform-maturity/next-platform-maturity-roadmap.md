Every major operational workflow must become:

- browser-proven;
- replay-proven;
- chronology-proven;
- provider-scoped;
- reconnect-safe.

### Required domains

- daily notes;
- safeguarding;
- missing episodes;
- return-home interviews;
- key work;
- documents;
- review/signoff;
- operational queues.

## Priority 2 — Canonical operational rendering

The frontend must converge onto:

- one chronology philosophy;
- one lifecycle philosophy;
- one queue philosophy;
- one evidence philosophy.

### Remove

- duplicated cards;
- duplicated summaries;
- fragmented operational views;
- dashboard clutter;
- fake operational visibility.

The platform should increasingly surface:

- recurring safeguarding themes;
- unresolved operational pressure;

- unresolved follow-up;

- predictive safeguarding AI;

- autonomous operational judgement.

- repeated summaries;
- clutter-heavy operational surfaces.

- predictive safeguarding scoring;

- AI-generated operational truth;
- pseudo-clinical AI behaviour.

- duplicate chronology systems;
