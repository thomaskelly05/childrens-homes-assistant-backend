# ORB Residential — Safeguarding Intelligence Convergence

## Objective

Create a single safeguarding reasoning layer inside:

# IndiCare Intelligence Spine

used by:

- ORB Residential
- IndiCare OS

without sharing operational evidence or runtime permissions.

---

# Current issue

Safeguarding logic currently exists across:

- assistant services
- ORB runtime helpers
- recording helpers
- response shaping
- operational flows

This creates:

- duplicated logic
- inconsistent responses
- fragmented escalation guidance
- inconsistent tone
- inconsistent safeguarding framing

---

# Target architecture

## Shared safeguarding reasoning layer

### Proposed runtime

# safeguarding_intelligence_service.py

Purpose:

- concern identification
- safeguarding-safe wording
- factual separation
- escalation thinking
- evidence clarity
- child-centred safeguarding framing
- uncertainty handling
- emotional de-escalation support

---

# Important architectural rule

The safeguarding reasoning layer is:

# shared intelligence.

NOT:

# shared operational access.

---

# ORB Residential behaviour

ORB Residential safeguarding support must:

- use user supplied information only
- explain evidence limitations clearly
- separate facts from assumptions
- encourage escalation through local procedures
- avoid pretending certainty
- avoid pretending chronology visibility

---

# IndiCare OS behaviour

IndiCare OS safeguarding support may:

- enrich from chronology
- enrich from incidents
- enrich from linked plans
- enrich from provider evidence
- support operational oversight

using:

# OperationalContextAdapter only.

---

# Shared safeguarding principles

All safeguarding outputs should:

- remain child-centred
- avoid inflammatory language
- separate observations from interpretation
- avoid diagnostic assumptions
- identify evidence gaps
- encourage professional escalation pathways
- support reflective thinking

- think clearly
- separate facts
- identify concerns
- identify uncertainty
- identify next-step considerations

## Recording support

- write factual concerns
- avoid emotionally loaded wording
- avoid unsafe assumptions
- improve evidence clarity

---

## Escalation support

Help adults think through:

- who may need informing
- urgency considerations
- evidence gaps
- safeguarding pathways

without replacing local policy.

## Emotional support

Help adults:

- regulate thinking during stress
- avoid panic escalation
- maintain clarity
- maintain child-centred reasoning
