# ORB Residential — Shift Builder

## Product role

Shift Builder is intended to become the core paid workflow inside ORB Residential.

It converts rough shift notes into structured residential-care intelligence and recording support.

---

# Problem

Residential staff often:

- finish shift exhausted
- struggle to structure records
- duplicate information across systems
- miss child voice
- miss chronology points
- miss safeguarding escalation considerations
- write overly punitive or vague recording
- struggle to prepare handovers

Shift Builder reduces this cognitive load.

---

# Standalone ORB rules

Shift Builder must:

- use only user supplied notes
- never imply access to live records
- never invent chronology or incidents
- identify evidence gaps clearly
- support therapeutic and child-centred language
- support reflective practice

---
