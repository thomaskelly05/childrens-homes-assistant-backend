# ORB Residential — Human Review and Safeguarding (Provider Trust Document)

## Human review

ORB outputs are **drafts and decision support only**. The product is designed to support workflows where a qualified professional reviews, edits, and approves content before use in records, supervision, or external communication.

Provider AI settings require acknowledgement that **human review is required** before enabling external AI processing.

## Safeguarding

ORB Residential:

- Does **not** replace child protection policies, LADO referral processes, or statutory recording requirements
- Does **not** monitor children in real time
- Does **not** store standalone child profiles or OS care records in the standalone product boundary
- May surface general safeguarding **guidance** — not case-specific determinations

Providers must ensure:

- Staff training on appropriate AI use
- Clear escalation routes for safeguarding concerns
- Local policies on recording conversations and consent (especially for Dictate audio features)

## Consent for audio

Dictate routes include consent confirmation for conversation/debrief transcription. Providers should align UI copy with local policy.

## Redaction

When enabled, redaction reduces identifiable content sent to external AI. Redaction is not perfect — treat outputs as sensitive until reviewed.

## Incident escalation

If AI output suggests immediate risk of harm, staff must follow existing emergency and safeguarding procedures — not ORB-specific workflows alone.

## Legal review

This document supports trust conversations; adapt for your policies and regulator expectations.
